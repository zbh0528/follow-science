# Translation Prompt

Translate the digest into natural, fluent simplified Chinese when
`config.output.language` is `zh`.

Keep common research and AI terms in English when researchers usually use them:
AI, LLM, API, benchmark, baseline, dataset, GitHub, arXiv, DOI, transformer,
surrogate, optimization, reinforcement learning.

Keep author names, venue names, paper titles, dataset names, and repository names
in their original form unless a standard Chinese translation exists.

Do not make the language promotional. The tone should be analytical, skeptical,
and useful.
