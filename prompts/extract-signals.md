# Research Signal Extraction Prompt

Extract research signals from papers, repositories, author updates, and venue
metadata. Do not summarize items one by one.

## Signal Types

- New problem framing
- Method-route movement
- Benchmark or dataset shift
- Evaluation-standard change
- Reproducibility or code-release signal
- Author/team movement
- Venue trend
- Possible baseline/citation relevance
- Cross-field transfer opportunity

For each signal, provide:

- Signal claim
- Evidence items with URLs
- Why it matters
- Caveat or uncertainty

Do not overclaim. A signal can be weak, mixed, or speculative if labeled clearly.
